ABOUT

Hello, I’m Batman, the silent guardian of Gotham City. When the city sleeps, I watch from the shadows. Crime and fear spread through these streets, but I refuse to let darkness win. I have trained my mind and body to become a symbol of justice. I don’t rely on superpowers, only discipline, intelligence, and determination. Every night I face dangerous criminals who threaten innocent lives. My mission is simple: protect the people and bring fear to those who bring harm. As long as Gotham needs me, the Dark Knight will always rise.

HISTORY

My story began in Gotham City, where I was born as Bruce Wayne. I grew up in a wealthy family with my parents, Thomas and Martha Wayne. One tragic night, I witnessed my parents being killed by a criminal in front of me. That painful moment changed my life forever and filled me with a deep desire for justice. As I grew older, I traveled the world to train my mind and body and learn the skills needed to fight crime. When I returned to Gotham, I chose the symbol of the bat to strike fear into the hearts of criminals. From that day on, I became Batman, the protector of this city. My mission has always been to defend the innocent and bring justice to Gotham.
